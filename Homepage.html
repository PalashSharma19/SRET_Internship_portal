About Us

The Sri Ramachandra Faculty of Engineering and Technology (SRET) is part of the Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research (SRIHER), located in Porur, Chennai. We offer cutting-edge programs in AI, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Cyber Security, IoT,Electronic and Communication, Medical Engineering, and Bioinformatics.

Our institution ensures a strong career foundation through excellent placement opportunities, internships, and industry collaborations. Students also have pathways to pursue **higher studies** such as MS and PhD programs globally.

With a world-class learning environment and experienced faculty, SRET prepares students to become future technology leaders.
